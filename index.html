FinSight Benchmark

A standalone AI-powered benchmarking tool that evaluates business performance using industry-based financial ratios.

The Problem

Many businesses calculate financial ratios but lack industry context to understand whether their performance is good or bad.

The Solution

FinSight Benchmark compares company ratios with industry averages and provides explanations and improvement advice.

Industries Covered

  • Travel & Tourism Services
  • E-commerce & Online Retail
  • Technology Startups (SaaS)
  • Hospitality (Hotels & Restaurants)
